Adipurush box office collection Day 3: Prabhas' film earns Rs 340 crore despite negative reviews.

Prabhas' highly anticipated film, Adipurush, is enjoying a great run in theatres. The film grossed over Rs 340 crore worldwide in its opening weekend and is still going strong at the box office. On Sunday, June 18, the film did great business in theatres and is said to have earned nearly Rs 64 crore in India. This Monday, June 19, is quite crucial for Adipurush and one has to wait and see if the film will be able to hold well during the weekdays
